Analytics

Cyberpunk 2077' sold over 13,7 million copies in 2020 with 830,387 all-time peak of people playing the game on Steam. According to a report shared by Reuters, the first quarter of this fiscal year has been tough for a CD Projekt that has failed to meet its own expectations: registers a 64.7% drop in net profits (about 8.87 million dollars at the exchange rate) due in large part to the problems that CP2077 is dragging on and on which it continues to work today.

Marketing

The total budget of Cyberpunk 2077 was PLN 1.2 billion (about USD 315 million). This is a huge amount, especially when compared to 2015's The Witcher 3, whose production and marketing campaign consumed PLN 306 million ($ 81 million).

Development

A new report looking into what went amiss with the rollout of Cyberpunk 2077 points to overconfidence from management at CD Projekt Red as a key problem, and reveals that “full development” of the game — announced in 2012— didn’t begin until 2016.

Support

CD Projekt Red has added modding tools and resources to Cyberpunk 2077 for players to adjust their experience. The developer announced the news on its website. “Tools will be continuously updated alongside with game patches to ensure compatibility,” the post reads.
